employee and their AI assistant. Produce a structured
summary that preserves

1. Decisions made, with their final state.
2. Tool calls and their results, including file paths
   and key numbers.
3. Open questions and unfinished tasks.
4. Persona and preferences the employee revealed.

as the conversation. Do not include pleasantries. Aim
for 200 to 500 words — long enough to retain detail,
short enough that one summary fits well below the recent
turns in the next LLM call's context.
